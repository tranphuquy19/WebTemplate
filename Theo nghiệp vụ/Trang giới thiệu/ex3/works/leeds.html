Leeds Beckett University

The website for modern students.

New name, new web

A new name was given to the Metropolitan University of Leeds: Leeds Beckett University. This new name required of a new website and a digital identity for a consistent communication. The result was a modern digital design and clean layout that engage with the young and modern students making the website a handy tool on a day-to-day basis.

Visit project

The complexity of its architecture information made the UX a key part of the project. We decided that the best option was to create a mobile first approach website. Our goal was to simplify the information making it the cleanest and the simplest as possible. Slick design and interactions helped to achieve this purpose.
