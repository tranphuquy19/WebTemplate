Website

Save the Children

Responsive multi-media scrollable website with editorial approach.

The "Signature Programmes" to everyone

Save the Children's Signature Programmes showcases how the charity helps young kids to preserve their basic rights such as health, basic education and, on top of everything, their own survival.

Originally created as a tool for their employees to access the programmes, this private web app soon became a public one. It was a success within the company and they decided to make it public. The goal then consisted in showing these programmes as an attractive source to get funds.

Moreover, we preserved the private log-in at the end of each programme. The site was fully controllable via CMS allowing Save the Children to customize the content whenever they need.

Get responsive, available everywhere.

With the creation of a responsive web app, we could show the stories behind these programmes in a compelling digital storytelling format. A full responsive approach was applied and all the multimedia assets were optimised for each device and screen sizes.

Being an international project, the web app was a multilingual and complete editorial storytelling digital experience. The set of texts, quotes, videos, interactive maps and background ambience sounds helped to achieve credibility and impact.
