The Resto
Restaurant

The perfect place to enjoy the life and food.

Resto Restaurant & Bar is a traditional European cuisine venue, serving the best dishes and drinks from across the range of European cuisines. A definitive neighborhood joint in Davis Square, Resto offers a laid-back dining experience in a casual setting to guests from all walks of life. Honest food and honest drinks set the foundation of our place's menu!

Since 1982

Resto is proud to be serving fine European dishes for more than 30 years!
