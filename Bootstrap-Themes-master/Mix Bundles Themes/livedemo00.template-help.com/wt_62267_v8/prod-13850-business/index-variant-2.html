Meet Our Team

Calvin Fitgerald

Founder, Managing Director

Calvins love for website development started in 1993 when at the age of 12 he learned HTML and secured his first dot-com. After graduating from the University of Minnesota in 2005, Calvin incorporated Quart as a way to advance his career, drive his passion for website development, and help other small business owners with their online presence.

Taylor Wilson

Senior Designer

Taylor joined the Quart team in August of 2016, shortly after relocating to Minneapolis from Chicago. He has been working in the sphere of web design for 9 years. He has extensive experience with creating responsive HTML websites that are built to increase sales in all industries. He believes quality websites are great for conveying important information.

Josh Wagner

He has a unique combination of database experience, network and software support skills.
