Ethan’s Vision in His Own Words

It is my pleasure to welcome you to Military School. At Military School, our goal is to help your son reach his goals. We offer many young men an opportunity that is very rare: a new start with a clean slate. New students have the opportunity to grow and become who they want to be, without the pressure or expectations of their past.

The whole Military School team will help your son to develop his character.

We strive to do this through :

  • Developing character
  • Academic ability
  • Guiding students
  • Assisting students
  • Athletics

Join us in moving forward to make “the world a better place because Military School boys lived.”
